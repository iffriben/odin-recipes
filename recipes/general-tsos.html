Healthy General Tso's Chicken

About This Recipe

I have never tried this recipe! But it comes from my friend Madi, who can be trusted with knowledge of all things food. I will be trying this recipe very soon.

Ingredients:

For the chicken:

  • 2 lbs chicken breast
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • salt
  • pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder

For the sauce:

  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 thumb ginger
  • 1 bunch scallions
  • 3 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp chicken stock
  • 2 tbsp shaoxing wine
  • 2 t sp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 4 tbsp honey or sugar
  • Sesame seeds

Instructions:

To prepare the chicken:

  1. Cut chicken into bite sized pieces.
  2. Coat in cornstarch and seasonings.
  3. Spray air fryer and chicken with cooking spray.
  4. Air fry at 400 degrees for 20 min, flipping halfway through.

To prepare the sauce:

  1. Saute garlic, ginger, and scallion bottoms in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Mix all other ingredients together in a measuring cup. Pour sauce into the pan and cook 1 min till bubbling and thickened.
  3. Coat chicken in sauce and scallion tops.
